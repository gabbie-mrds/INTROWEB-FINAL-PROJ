KNOW MORE ABOUT US!

In order to share the ambiance and view that we were able to discover in La Union, we opened this coffee shop! Not only that, but we also want you to enjoy our GREAT COFFEE and DESSERTS.

Do we have other branches?
Unfortunately, we don't. But don't worry! We are continuously exploring to find a new place where it'll have the same vibe in other location.
Do we bake our own desserts?
Sure we do! We proudly prepare them ourselves. In order to provide you guys more dessert options, we actually change our dessert menu once a month. Keep an eye out for the monthly offers we have.
Do you guys have other beverages beside coffee?
We are pleased to inform you that we do provide drinks other than coffee. Also, we provide seasonal beverages in addition to our signature drinks.

WHAT'S SPECIAL ABOUT OUR COFFEE?

Wanna know our secret? We actually use local coffee beans that are all the way from Baguio! So, we support our local goods and our local farmers.

And until now, we are still exploring other local coffee beans so that more people would appreciate our own goods more. Supporting locally produced goods helps to increase the appreciation for our local farmers.

Also, if you have local coffee bean recommendations, we are very much interested to know about it! So you may email us for you to share it.
